About Me

I'm a Bachelor of Engineering graduate from Germany with a deep passion for creating inclusive and accessible web applications. I believe that the web should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities or the devices they use.

My expertise spans across web development and UI/UX design, allowing me to create user-centered experiences that are both beautiful and functional. I'm proficient in Figma and follow accessibility best practices in all my projects.

I'm committed to building web solutions that prioritize accessibility, usability, and inclusive design principles.
